Since 1969

Hotel Catamaran was born, at the end of the 60s, as a strategic stop for the nautical charters business, started by Louisa and Kevin Lucas, from Rio Dulce to the Belize Keys. Soon, amazed by the charm of the place, the tourist looks spend more time in Río Dulce, requiring a superior site to stay. Fifty years later, our island continues to be the ideal stop for travelers who enjoy Rio Dulce as a final destination, or for those who are on the way to other destinations of the Guatemalan Caribbean.

Welcome!

Welcome to Catamaran Island Hotel & Marina, a tropical paradise in the Caribbean Guatemalan.

Our island, located between the great Lake of Izabal and the Río Dulce canyon, offers you the Best views and sunsets from the balcony of your room. Depending on your preference, adventurous tourism or just relax under a palm tree, Hotel Catamaran is your place. Our qualified staff will be at your disposal to help you in Whatever you need. Do not forget to order our famous Margaritas and Pinas Coladas in our bar located at the edge of the river.

Thank you for your preference and we hope that your stay will be unforgettable.
